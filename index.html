Our identity

In the general economy, businesses and individuals exchange resources, in order to survive. Unfortunately, every business has its own segmentation, meaning that there is an uncertainty of unmet needs.

Governmental institutions play an important role in meeting public needs, as they have relatively more resources than businesses. Unfortunately, they only have few years of term limits to actualize programs.

Educational institutions produce the next practitioners and academicians, which will solve the social problems. Unfortunately, their schedules are tight with deadlines and their collaborations are bounded with faculties.

Aktivis FOSS is born to fill the three parties' limitations. We build a network where everyone can flexibly solve real problems in Indonesia, especially for public and education sector.

Our objective

Digitally assist governmental and educational institutions in Indonesia in addressing public unmet needs.

Our action set

  • Identify governmental and educational problems that haven't been solved by businesses.
  • Collaborate with experts from multiple disciplines to create standard of expectations.
  • Manage an open innovation network where ones can exchange ideas concretely.
  • Build maintainable digital products that are free and humane to use.

Our rationale

In the end, we are humans. We may have our collective objective. But, we also do such things so we can get personal recognition, which open us to business opportunities.
